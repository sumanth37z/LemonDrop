## 📖 What is LemonDrop?

LemonDrop is a free disposable email service that lets you create temporary email addresses instantly — no signup, no credit card, no personal information required.

Use it to:
- Sign up for websites without revealing your real email
- Avoid spam in your personal inbox
- Test email functionality during development
- Receive verification emails anonymously

Emails automatically delete after **1 hour** to keep your privacy intact.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Instant email address** — Generated automatically on every visit
- **No signup required** — Just open the site and start using it
- **Full HTML email support** — Images, icons, and styled emails render correctly
- **Auto-refresh inbox** — Checks for new emails every 5 seconds
- **Split panel UI** — Professional email client layout (like Gmail)
- **Colored avatars** — Unique avatar for every sender
- **Mobile responsive** — Works perfectly on all screen sizes
- **Session-based privacy** — New address every time you open a new tab
- **Auto-cleanup** — Emails deleted after 1 hour automatically
- **Custom domain** — Emails arrive at `@lemondrop.qzz.io`
- **100% Free** — No hidden costs, no subscriptions
